# Lorraine, Wandering Warrior — An In-Depth Card Theory

Grand Archive is a game full of beautifully designed champions, but few have captured the community's imagination quite like **Lorraine, Wandering Warrior**. She's a midrange powerhouse with an elegant design philosophy: she rewards players who maintain board presence and punishes those who overextend.

## What Makes Lorraine Tick

At her core, Lorraine is a *tempo* champion. Her ability to generate card advantage from sustained attacks means she thrives in games that go long. But unlike other value-oriented champions, she has a natural clock — if you're not converting her Floating Memory efficiently, you're leaving resources on the table.

> "Every card you don't use on your turn is a card your opponent gets to play around." — a floor judge at a Grand Archive Regional

### The Tempo Equation

The key insight with Lorraine is understanding that **board presence = future options**. When you control the board:

1. Your opponent has to react to *you*
2. You dictate the pace of trading
3. Lorraine's ability triggers more consistently

This creates a virtuous cycle — control the board, generate advantage, overwhelm with threats.

## Deckbuilding Philosophy

When building around Lorraine, I recommend thinking in three layers:

### Layer 1: The Engine

Your engine cards are the ones that enable Lorraine's ability repeatedly. Look for cards with:

- Low combat cost that can be reliably floated
- Efficient attack values relative to memory cost
- Cards that threaten the champion directly

### Layer 2: The Answers

Every great Lorraine deck needs answers — cards that handle the problems your engine can't. This includes:

- Removal for threatening enemies
- Recursion for when your key cards get milled
- Disruption for opposing combo lines

### Layer 3: The Finishers

Lorraine doesn't win from a single big turn. Her finishers are *inevitability* cards — cards that become better as the game goes on and your board state grows.

## The Meta Positioning

As of early 2026, Lorraine sits in a fascinating spot in the meta. She's not the most powerful champion in terms of raw explosive potential, but her **consistency** is unmatched. In a best-of-three match, a skilled Lorraine pilot will outlast almost any single-game powerhouse.

The champions she struggles against are those that:

- **Disrupt her memory generation** — removing access to floated cards cripples her engine
- **Go wide faster** — flooding the board before Lorraine can establish her rhythm
- **Have alternative win conditions** — champions that can close games without needing to trade favorably

## Conclusion

Lorraine rewards patience and planning. If you're the type of player who enjoys optimizing every resource and seeing your game plan slowly come together over multiple turns, she's your champion.
